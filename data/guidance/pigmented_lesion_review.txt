Pigmented lesion safety guidance:

Prompt clinician or dermatology review is appropriate for lesions that are new, changing, asymmetric, have irregular borders, show multiple colors, are larger than other spots, itch, bleed, crust, grow quickly, or look different from the person's other moles. The ABCDE framework can help describe concerning features, but it cannot confirm whether a lesion is benign or malignant.
